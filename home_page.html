The Taste From Sea

Welcome to Tuyet Restaurant, a place where seafood is celebrated in its most authentic, natural form. Our philosophy is simple: fresh catch, honest cooking, and unforgettable flavors. We partner with trusted fishermen and sustainable farms to ensure every ingredient on your plate reflects the true essence of the ocean.
